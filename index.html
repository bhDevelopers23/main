About Pizza

Pizza is a beloved and versatile dish that originated in Naples, Italy. It typically consists of a round, flat dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings, baked in an oven. The simplicity of its ingredients belies the complex and satisfying flavors it offers. The crust can range from thin and crispy to thick and chewy, appealing to a diverse range of preferences. Pizza has evolved over time and has become a global phenomenon, with countless regional variations and creative toppings. Popular choices include classics like Margherita, pepperoni, and Hawaiian, as well as gourmet options with unique ingredients. Whether enjoyed at a pizzeria, ordered for delivery, or homemade, pizza remains a universally loved comfort food that brings people together. Its enduring popularity reflects its ability to adapt to different tastes while retaining its fundamental appeal.

Where to Order the Best Pizza

Now that you know what’s cooking in the world of pizza, it’s time to place your order. While you can get pizza just about anywhere, some cities just do it better than others. These are the 6 most pizza-obsessed cities by the number of pies ordered.

Dirty Water Dough (Boston, MA)

If you’re looking for delicious slice of Boston-style pizza, then Dirty Water Dough is for you. Don’t be put off by their name, this is a solid Boston favorite.

Couch Tomato Catering (Philadelphia, PA)

For pies for a big crowd, there’s none better than Couch Tomato Catering. This long-standing Philly operation mixes classics like cheese and pepperoni with new favorites like buffalo chicken. Try the Florentine, for a delightful mix of spinach and cheese. .

Listrani’s Italian Gourmet (Washington, DC)

Listrani’s Mclean Italian Gourmet has been serving pizza lovers in D.C. since 2002. From mini pizzas to big pan pizzas, Listrani’s has got it all.

Palio’s Pizza Café (Dallas, TX)

Palio’s Pizza Café offers a lengthy list of gourmet pies with artisanal ingredients and clever names. The Nutty Chicken is topped with roasted cashews, chicken, mushroom, and spinach. And the Mean Green is made with Alfredo, pesto, spinach, bacon, feta, mozzarella.

Pizza Capri (Chicago, IL)

Have you ever had a pizza topped with Kalamata olives and sun-dried tomatoes? Or have you tried rosemary potatoes and garlic, or even pesto and shrimp on your pizza? Pizza Capri dares to go there. These quirky, tasty combos are the highlights of this Chicago institution.

California Pizza Kitchen (Los Angeles, CA)

There are so many different pizzas at California Pizza Kitchen, no wonder it’s a favorite all over the country. But if you’re looking for one of the most popular types of pizza, try the Original BBQ Chicken Pizza. It’s made with smoked gouda, red onions, cilantro, and a tangy BBQ sauce that you won’t forget.
